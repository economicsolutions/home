Who We Are

Techno Electric and Engineering Company Pvt. Ltd. (TEEC), based in Kathmandu, Nepal, is a forward-thinking technology and engineering enterprise committed to sustainable and socially responsible business practices. We specialize in importing and distributing a wide range of electrical and electronic products from leading global brands.

Our core offerings include laptops, televisions, digital signage systems, large format displays (LFDs), smart boards for education and advertising, diesel and gas analyzers, and more. We are particularly focused on introducing and promoting solutions that help reduce environmental impact and support community well-being—while generating sustainable profits.
